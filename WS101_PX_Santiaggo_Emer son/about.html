Who I Am

Hi! I'm Emer Son Santiago, a passionate Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Our Lady of the Sacred Heart College of Ojcet – Guimba. I am deeply fascinated by the intersection of technology and human experience — specifically how thoughtful design and clean code can transform the way people interact with digital tools.

Growing up in an era of rapid digital transformation, I've always been captivated by how a few lines of code can power something truly meaningful. That curiosity turned into a calling, and now I spend my days learning, building, and improving one project at a time.

My Academic Journey

Currently pursuing my BSIT degree, my coursework spans across web development, object-oriented programming, database systems, networking fundamentals, and human-computer interaction. Every subject has added another layer to my understanding of what it means to build reliable, user-centric technology.

As a member of the Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS), I also engage with a community of like-minded peers who share a love for computing and innovation — attending seminars, participating in competitions, and collaborating on tech-driven initiatives.
